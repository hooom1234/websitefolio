# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Tech recruiters, potential clients, and hiring managers looking for developer talent.

## Product Purpose
- Showcase software development skills, projects, and work timeline to land jobs/projects.
- Build a personal brand and share developer/coding experiences.
- Highlight creative frontend designs, interactive micro-animations, and experimental features.

## Brand Personality
- Creative, tech-forward, and professional.
- Vibrant but balanced (uses deep dark backgrounds with custom neon purple/cyan accents).

## Anti-references
- Boring, static Resume templates.
- Over-cluttered or overstimulating layouts that disrupt readability.
- Generic "AI-generated" designs: boring flat gray cards, or the standard saturated beige/cream (`--sand`, `--cream`) backgrounds.

## Design Principles
- **Creative Elegance**: Integrate interactive animations (like typewriter effects, hover animations, scroll transitions) while maintaining a highly professional tone.
- **Visual Distinction**: Steer away from generic boilerplate layout structures and templates. Use curated color palettes (like deep slate/purple) and bespoke components.
- **Readability & Hierarchy**: Ensure the information hierarchy is clear so recruiters can find key skills, experiences, and contact details in under 10 seconds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Responsive layout across mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports.
- Clear contrast ratios matching WCAG AA standard (body text ≥4.5:1 contrast against backgrounds).
- Standard web accessibility best practices.
